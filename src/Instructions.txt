  This is a turn-based bullet hell where time moves only when you move.

  Player (+):
  WASD to move
  Avoid numbers, mines, explosions

  Numbers (1, 2, 3, 4, 5):
  Number x falls down x tiles per turn
    Ex: 3 will fall 3 tiles per turn

  Mines (#):
  Formed from numbers colliding with each another
  Will explode when it collides with numbers
  Does not fall down, stays in place
  Detonates in a 2 tile radius
